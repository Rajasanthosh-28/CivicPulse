Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Navigate to Submit Complaint from the left menu. Select the relevant category (e.g., Roads, Electricity, Water Supply), choose the urgency level, provide a clear descriptive title, enter exact location landmarks, and optionally upload supporting photographic evidence (up to 10MB). Upon submission, you will receive a unique tracking ID such as CP-20260708-1042.

Resolution SLAs depend on grievance priority and category:
  • Urgent / Emergency: Within 24 hours (e.g., live wire hazards, sewage overflow)
  • High Priority: Within 3 working days (e.g., major street light outage, pipeline burst)
  • Medium / Low Priority: Within 7 working days (e.g., garbage collection schedules, road pothole patching)
