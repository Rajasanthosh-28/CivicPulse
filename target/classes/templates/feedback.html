How We Use Your Feedback

CivicPulse operates under the Citizen Service Charter. Every evaluation submitted by verified citizens directly affects departmental ratings and officer performance metrics.

  • Monthly Quality Audit by Municipal Commissioner
  • Re-opening of unresolved grievances rated below 2 stars
  • Recognition awards for high-performing civic divisions

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I modify my feedback later? Once submitted, ratings are locked into the transparency ledger to ensure data integrity.
Is my identity confidential? Assigned ground officers can see your review to address specific technical complaints, but personal contact information remains protected.
